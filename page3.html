Aims/Objectives of my Final Year Project

This project examines the art of the remix, how listeners perceive a remix, and also how they perceive genres. The project will be looking into why music matters so much in our lives, and why it can change listener’s emotions and feelings when listening to a piece.

This project will include an original composition, which will then be remixed three times. The Ambient and Trap remix has been carried out by the author and the techno remix has been produced by another artist. The feedback has been gathered by listening tests and questionnaires, which show that the main composition and trap remix were the equal favourites and the ambient remix was the least favourite. These remixes aimed to show readers and listeners that the possibilities are endless with one piece of original music.

When getting feedback off users, it appeared the main concept of the project was appreciated. Users enjoyed listening to the same track but remixed into different genres and highlighted how this project provided insight into diverse genres and remixing, and it’s importance in electronic music production.
